History

From day one, our mission has been simple: create thrilling, safe, and memorable river experiences for everyone—from first-timers to seasoned paddlers. Over the years, we've guided thousands of adventurers through Class I to Class V rapids, expanded our fleet, trained world-class guides, and established ourselves as a cornerstone of the local outdoor community.

We're proud to have built a company that honors both the adrenaline rush and the serene beauty of the river. As we grow, we remain grounded in our roots—committed to environmental stewardship, community connection, and sharing the magic of whitewater with every guest who grabs a paddle and joins us on the journey.
